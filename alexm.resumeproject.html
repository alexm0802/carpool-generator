Background

I am a coxswain for the Lakeside Men's Crew Team. This primarily involves yelling at sweaty boys. I do this five to six times a week, pretty much year-round. Why? Because there's nothing better than steering a straight course across flat water while your boat is rowing well, and every practice helps us get there. Our motto? "Row Fast, Don't Suck".
